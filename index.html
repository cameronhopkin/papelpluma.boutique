Nuestra Historia

Where Two Worlds Meet

Papel Pluma was born from a love letter to heritage — the bold geometry of Taíno art, the flowing script of Spanish poetry, and the quiet precision of Japanese papercraft.

We believe that every notebook, every pen, every piece of washi tape is an invitation to slow down, to create something by hand, and to carry a piece of culture with you wherever you write.

This isn't just a store. It's a mesa — a table where you sit, where you write, where you belong.
